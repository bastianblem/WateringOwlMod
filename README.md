# Watering Owl Mod

This mod adds an automated water-fetching owl to *Ale and Tale Tavern*.
This helper owl refills your dishwashers automatically by filling up buckets from it's inventory at the well. Then it goes back to fill up your dishwashers and waits in his house after for a new task.

## Features
* **Automated Refilling:** Owls collect water from the well and refill empty dishwashers.
* **New Item:** Adds a blue "Water Owl House" (ID: 8766) to the shop.
* **Integrated AI:** Includes custom states for nest sitting, water fetching, and refilling.
